## Tips and Callouts

Use callouts to highlight important information that shouldn't be missed.

<Callout type="tip" title="Best Time to Visit">
  Seasonal advice or practical tips go here. Keep it to 2-3 sentences max.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  General context or fun facts. The title prop is optional.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Heads Up">
  Warnings about reservations, costs, seasonal closures, or anything readers should plan for.
</Callout>

## Standard Markdown Still Works

Everything you can do in `.md` files works in `.mdx` too:

- **Bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~
- [Links](https://example.com)
- `inline code` and fenced code blocks
- Blockquotes, lists, images, horizontal rules

> "Blockquotes work exactly the same way."
