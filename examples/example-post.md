Opening paragraph — hook the reader with something specific and interesting. Avoid generic introductions like "In today's world..." or "Have you ever wondered...". Jump straight into the story or insight.

## First Section Heading

Write in short paragraphs. Each paragraph should make one clear point. Blog readers scan before they read, so keep things digestible.

Use **bold** for emphasis on key phrases, and *italics* for softer emphasis or titles. Don't overdo either.

## Lists Work Well for Actionable Content

1. **Tip one** — Brief explanation of why this matters.
2. **Tip two** — Keep each item to one or two sentences.
3. **Tip three** — End with the strongest or most surprising point.
