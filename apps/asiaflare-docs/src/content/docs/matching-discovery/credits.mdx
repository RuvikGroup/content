Credits are the in-app currency used to unlock certain actions on AsiaFlare, like sending messages to people you haven't matched with yet, or using Super Likes.

## What you can do with credits

| Action | Credits used |
|--------|-------------|
| Message someone before matching | ✓ |
| Send a Super Like | ✓ |
| Send a Super Message | ✓ |

## How to earn credits

**Spin the Wheel**

Every day you can spin the wheel for a chance to win free credits. Open the app and tap the **Wheel** icon to spin.

<img src="/images/credits-wheel.png" alt="Spin the wheel screen" />

**Purchase credits**

You can purchase credits directly through the app. Go to your profile and tap **Manage Subscriptions & Credits** to see available credit packages.

**Subscription**

Premium and VIP subscribers receive credits as part of their subscription.

## Checking your credit balance

Your current credit balance is shown in the app. You'll also see how many credits an action costs before you confirm it, so you're never surprised.
