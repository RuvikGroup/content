When you first join AsiaFlare, you'll be guided through a short setup process before you can start discovering people. The more you fill in, the better your matches will be.

![Screenshot 20260424 114848 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_114848_AsiaFlare.jpg)

## What you'll be asked to fill in

**Basic details**

- Your name and nickname
- Date of birth
- Gender and who you're interested in

**Your photos**

- Upload up to 6 photos. Your first photo will be your main profile image — choose a clear, well-lit shot of your face.

**About you**

- A bio (optional but strongly recommended)
- Height, body type
- Relationship type you're looking for
- Religion
- Drinking and smoking status

**Your preferences**

- The countries and regions you'd like to match with
- Age range and distance filters

## Tips for a strong profile

- **Add at least 4 photos** — profiles with more photos get significantly more matches
- **Write a bio** — even a few sentences makes a big difference. See our [bio writing guide](/writing-bio) for tips.
- **Be accurate** — honesty upfront leads to better connections

## You can update everything later

Nothing you set during onboarding is permanent. You can edit any part of your profile at any time from the profile tab.

<Aside type="tip">
If you exit the setup process part way through, your progress is saved. You can pick up where you left off when you return.
</Aside>

<Card title="How to choose great profile photos" icon="camera" href="/your-profile/profile-photos">
  - **Show Your Face:** Skip the sunglasses, hats, and masks.
  - **Go Solo:** Avoid group shots. Stand out on your own.
  - **Mix It Up:** Include both close-ups and full-body shots.
  - **Keep It Natural:** Ditch the bathroom mirror selfies. Natural lighting wins.
  - **Dress Your Best:** Wear clothes that fit well and show your style.
  - **Look Approachable:** Drop the tough look. Smile and look warm.
  - **Upload 5 to 8 Photos:** Give people a real sense of who you are.
</Card>
