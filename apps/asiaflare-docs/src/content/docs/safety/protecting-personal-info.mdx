You don't need to share personal information to make a genuine connection. Being thoughtful about what you share — and when — keeps you in control.

<img src="/images/guide-personal-info-hero.png" alt="Privacy settings in the app" />

## What not to share early on

Before you've met someone in person and built real trust, avoid sharing:

- **Your home address or exact neighbourhood**
- **Your workplace name and location**
- **Financial information** of any kind
- **Copies of ID or personal documents**
- **Your personal phone number** — use in-app messaging until you're comfortable

There's no rush. A person worth meeting will respect that you take your time.

## Using the app's privacy features

AsiaFlare has built-in privacy controls you can use to manage your visibility:

- **Location** — the app shows your general distance from other users, not your exact location. You control how precise this is in your privacy settings.
- **Incognito mode** — browse profiles without appearing in other people's feeds or showing read receipts
- **Block** — immediately removes someone from your experience and prevents any further contact

## Sharing photos outside the app

Be cautious about sending photos directly outside the app, particularly photos that contain identifiable information (your home, your car number plate, landmarks near where you live).

## If your information is misused

If someone is using your personal information in a way that makes you feel unsafe:

1. **Block and report the user** in the app immediately
2. **Contact local authorities** if you feel at risk
3. **Reach out to support** via our [contact form](https://asiaflareapp.com/contact) and our team will help
