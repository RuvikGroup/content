AsiaFlare shows you profiles one at a time. You decide who you're interested in, and when two people both like each other, it's a match.

![Screenshot 20260424 123325 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_123325_AsiaFlare.jpg)

## The basic flow

You'll see profile cards in your discover feed. For each person, you have a few options:

| Action | How to do it | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Like** | Swipe right or tap ❤️ | Tells them you're interested |
| **Dislike** | Swipe left or tap ✕ | Passes on this profile |
| **Super Like** | Tap the ⭐ button | Sends a special like that stands out |
| **Super Message** | Tap the 💬 button | Sends a highlighted message before matching |

## When you get a match

If someone you liked also likes you back, you'll both get a match notification and a celebratory screen showing your profiles together.

![Screenshot 20260424 130407 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_130407_AsiaFlare.jpg)

From the match screen you can send your first message straight away.

## Super Likes

A Super Like lets someone know you're especially interested — their profile will show that you super liked them. Super Likes use credits.

## Who you see

The profiles shown to you are based on your preference settings — age range, distance, and who you're interested in. Updating your preferences changes who appears in your feed.

## Profiles you've already seen

Once you've liked or disliked someone, they won't appear in your feed again. If you change your mind about someone you've disliked, this can be reversed with certain subscription tiers.
