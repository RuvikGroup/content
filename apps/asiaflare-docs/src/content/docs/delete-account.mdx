Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your content, matches, messages, and profile information will be removed.

<Aside type="caution">
This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.
</Aside>

## Before you delete

If you're unsure, consider reaching out to support first — we may be able to help with whatever is making you want to leave.

You do **not** need to change your password or cancel your subscription before deleting your account. You will be asked to confirm before deletion proceeds.

## How to delete your account

<Steps>

1. **Open Settings**

   Tap your profile in the navigation bar, then navigate to **Privacy**.

       ![Arrow Image (7)](/images/arrow-image-\(7\).jpg)

2. **Tap Delete Account**

   Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section and tap **Delete Account**.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 135912 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_135912_AsiaFlare.jpg)

3. **Confirm deletion**

   Type `DELETE` in the text field to confirm, then tap **Confirm Delete**.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 135926 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_135926_AsiaFlare.jpg)

</Steps>

## What happens after deletion

- Your profile is immediately removed and no longer visible to other users
- All your matches and conversations are permanently deleted
- Any active subscription is not automatically cancelled — cancel it separately through the App Store or Google Play to avoid future charges

## Need help?

Contact support before deleting if you're experiencing an issue we might be able to resolve.
