AsiaFlare has two paid subscription tiers — Premium and VIP — that unlock additional features beyond the free experience.

## What's included

| Feature | Free | Premium |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Browse profiles | ✓ | ✓ |
| Like profiles | ✓ | ✓ |
| Send messages to matches | ✓ | ✓ |
| See who liked you | ✓ | ✓ |
| See who viewed your profile | — | ✓ |
| Send unlimited messages | — | ✓ |
| Advanced filters | — | ✓ |
| Incognito mode | — | ✓ |
|  |  |  |

## Subscription options

Premium is available on monthly or annual plans. Annual plans offer a significant saving compared to paying month by month.

## How to subscribe

<Steps>

1. **Open the subscription screen**

   Tap any locked Premium feature, or go to your profile and tap **Manage Subscriptions & Credits**.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 131641 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_131641_AsiaFlare.jpg)

2. **Choose a plan**

   Choose your billing period.

3. **Confirm your purchase**

   Complete the purchase through the App Store or Google Play. Your subscription activates immediately.

</Steps>

## Cancelling or managing your subscription

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
