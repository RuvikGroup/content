Subscriptions are billed and managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). To stop future charges, you need to cancel directly through the platform where you subscribed.

<Aside type="caution">
Cancelling through the app store stops future renewals but does not provide a refund for the current billing period. You will retain access to your subscription until the end of the paid period.
</Aside>

## Cancel on iOS (App Store)

<Steps>

1. **Open Settings on your iPhone**

   Go to **Settings** → tap your **Apple ID** at the top of the screen.

       <img src="/images/cancel-ios-settings.png" alt="iPhone Settings with Apple ID at top" />

2. **Open Subscriptions**

   Tap **Subscriptions** to see all your active and expired subscriptions.

       <img src="/images/cancel-ios-subscriptions.png" alt="Apple ID subscriptions screen" />

3. **Select your subscription**

   Find and tap the app subscription you want to cancel.

       <img src="/images/cancel-ios-select.png" alt="Subscription detail screen" />

4. **Tap Cancel Subscription**

   Tap **Cancel Subscription** and confirm when prompted.

       <img src="/images/cancel-ios-confirm.png" alt="Cancel subscription confirmation" />

</Steps>

## Cancel on Android (Google Play)

<Steps>

1. **Open the Google Play Store**

   Open the **Google Play Store** app on your Android device.

       <img src="/images/cancel-android-play.png" alt="Google Play Store app" />

2. **Open your subscriptions**

   Tap your **profile icon** in the top-right corner, then go to **Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions**.

       <img src="/images/cancel-android-subscriptions.png" alt="Google Play subscriptions screen" />

3. **Select your subscription**

   Find and tap the subscription you want to cancel.

       <img src="/images/cancel-android-select.png" alt="Subscription detail screen" />

4. **Tap Cancel Subscription**

   Tap **Cancel subscription** and follow the prompts to confirm.

       <img src="/images/cancel-android-confirm.png" alt="Cancel subscription confirmation" />

</Steps>

## Cancel a web subscription

If you subscribed via the web, manage your subscription from within the app under **Settings → Manage Subscriptions & Credits**.

<img src="/images/cancel-web-manage.png" alt="Manage Subscriptions screen in app" />

## After cancellation

- Your Premium or VIP access remains active until the end of the current billing period
- You will not be charged again after cancellation
- Your account and all data are preserved — only the paid features revert to free tier
- If you change your mind, you can resubscribe at any time from the app

## Still seeing charges?

If you're still being charged after cancelling, verify that the cancellation was confirmed in the App Store or Google Play. Contact support if the issue persists.
