First dates can be nerve-wracking. Here are five tips to help you make a great impression on LatinFlare:

1. **Be authentic** — Be yourself from the start.
2. **Choose a relaxed venue** — Coffee or a casual meal works great.
3. **Ask questions** — Show genuine curiosity about their life.
4. **Put your phone away** — Give them your full attention.
5. **Be respectful of cultural nuances** — LatinFlare connects people across diverse cultures.
