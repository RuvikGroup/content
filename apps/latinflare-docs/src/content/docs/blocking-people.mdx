Blocking a user removes them from your experience entirely. They won't be able to see your profile, message you, or appear in your matches or discover feed.

## How to block someone

You can block a user from their profile or from a chat conversation.

### From a profile

<Steps>

1. **Open the user's profile**

   Tap on the user's profile card to open their full profile.

2. **Open the actions menu**

   Tap the menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner of the profile.

### From a chat

<Steps>

1. **Open the conversation**

   Navigate to your messages and open the chat with the user you want to block.

2. **Tap the user's name or the menu icon**

   Tap the user's name in the chat header or the menu icon (⋮) to open options.

       ![Arrow Image (6)](/images/arrow-image-\(6\).jpg)

3. **Tap Block User**

   Select **Block User** and confirm.

</Steps>

## What happens when you block someone

- The user is immediately removed from your matches, messages, discover feed, and all other lists
- Any existing conversation with them is deleted
- They will not be notified that they have been blocked
- You will not appear in their discover feed or matches either

## Viewing your blocked users

You can see a list of everyone you've blocked under **Settings → Blocked Users**.

![Blocked users list screen](/images/blocked-users-list.png)

## How to unblock someone

<Steps>

1. **Go to Blocked Users**

   Open **Settings → Blocked Users** to see your blocked list.

2. **Tap Unblock**

   Find the user and tap **Unblock**. They will become visible to you again and can appear in your discover feed.

       ![Unblock user option](/images/unblock-user.png)

</Steps>
