If you've reinstalled LatinFlare or switched to a new device, your subscription should restore automatically when you sign in with the same account. If it doesn't, you can restore it manually.

## Automatic restore

When you open the app and sign in, your subscription status is checked automatically. In most cases your Premium or VIP access will be restored without you needing to do anything.

## Manual restore

If your subscription isn't showing after signing in:

<Steps>

1. **Go to your profile**

   Tap the profile tab and open **Settings**.

2. **Tap Manage Subscriptions & Credits**

   This opens the subscription management screen.

       <img src="/images/restore-manage-screen.png" alt="Manage Subscriptions screen" />

3. **Tap Restore Purchases**

   Tap **Restore Purchases**. The app will check your App Store or Google Play account for any active or previous subscriptions.

       <img src="/images/restore-button.png" alt="Restore Purchases button" />

</Steps>

## Things to check if restore doesn't work

- Make sure you're signed in to the **same Apple ID or Google account** you used to originally purchase the subscription
- Make sure your subscription is still **active** — expired subscriptions cannot be restored
- Check your App Store or Google Play to confirm the subscription status

## Still having issues?

Contact support via our [contact form](https://latinflareapp.com/contact) and include the email address associated with your account. We'll look into it for you.
