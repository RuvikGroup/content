You can control which notifications LatinFlare sends you — both push notifications on your device and email notifications.

## What you can control

| Notification | Push | Email |
| --- | --- | --- |
| New messages | ✓ | ✓ |
| New likes | ✓ | ✓ |
| New matches | ✓ | ✓ |
| Profile views | ✓ | ✓ |

## How to update your notification settings

<Steps>

1. **Open Settings**

   Tap your Profile icon, then go to **Notifications Preferences**.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 131910 LatinFlare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_131910_LatinFlare.jpg)

2. **Toggle notifications on or off**

   Use the toggles next to each notification type to turn push and email notifications on or off independently.

</Steps>

## Enabling push notifications

If you've disabled push notifications for LatinFlare at the device level, a banner will appear at the top of the notifications settings screen. Tap **Enable Push Notifications** to be taken to your device settings where you can turn them back on.

![Arrow Image (4)](/images/arrow-image-\(4\).jpg)

<Aside type="tip">
Keeping match and message notifications on means you'll never miss a conversation — quick replies lead to much better conversations.
</Aside>
