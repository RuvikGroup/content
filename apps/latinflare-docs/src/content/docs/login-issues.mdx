Having trouble getting into your account? Here are the most common causes and how to fix them.

## Forgot your password

<Steps>

1. **Tap Forgot Password**

   On the login screen, tap the **Forgot Password** link below the sign-in button.

       ![Forgot password link on login screen](/images/login-forgot-password.png)

2. **Enter your email address**

   Enter the email address associated with your account and tap submit.

3. **Check your inbox**

   You'll receive a password reset email. Check your spam or junk folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
If the email doesn't arrive, make sure you're using the correct email address and that the email hasn't been filtered as spam.
</Aside>

## Signed up with Apple, Google, or Facebook

If you originally signed in using a social account, you can't log in with an email and password — you need to use the same social login button you used when you signed up.

On the login screen, tap:

- **Sign in with Apple** — if you signed up with your Apple ID
- **Sign in with Google** — if you signed up with your Google account
- **Sign in with Facebook** — if you signed up with Facebook

## Your password isn't being accepted

Passwords must be at least 8 characters. If you're sure you're entering the right password but it isn't working, use the **Forgot Password** flow to reset it.

## App says your account doesn't exist

Make sure you're entering the email address you signed up with. If you're not sure which email you used, try each one you might have used, or contact support.

## Sign in with Apple issues

On iOS, if Sign in with Apple isn't working:

- Check that you're signed in to your Apple ID in device **Settings → Apple ID**
- Make sure you haven't hidden your email from LatinFlare in your Apple ID settings (Settings → Apple ID → Password & Security → Apps Using Apple ID)

## Still can't get in?

Contact our support team via the [contact form](https://latinflareapp.com/contact) with your account email address and a brief description of what's happening. We'll get you back in.
