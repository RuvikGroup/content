Your photos are the first thing people see. You can add up to 6 photos to your profile and update them at any time.

## Adding a photo

<Steps>

1. **Open your profile**

   Tap the profile tab to open your profile page.

       ![Arrow Image](/images/arrow-image.jpg)

2. **Tap the + button**

   Tap the **\+** button in your photo gallery to open your device's photo library.

       ![Arrow Image (1)](/images/arrow-image-\(1\).jpg)

3. **Select and crop your photo**

   Choose a photo from your library. The photo editor will open so you can crop and adjust it before uploading.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 120445 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_120445_LatinFlare.jpg)

4. **Confirm the upload**

   Tap confirm to add the photo to your profile.

</Steps>

## Removing a photo

Tap the remove button on any photo in your gallery to delete it. Your first photo is your main profile image — if you remove it, the next photo in your gallery automatically becomes your main photo.

![Arrow Image (2)](/images/arrow-image-\(2\).jpg)

## Reordering photos

Press and hold a photo, then drag it to a new position in your gallery. The first photo in your gallery is always the one people see first.

<Aside type="tip">
Put your best, clearest photo first. It's the one shown on your profile card in the discover feed and makes the biggest impact on whether someone taps on your profile.
</Aside>

## How many photos should you have?

You can add up to 6 photos. Profiles with 4 or more photos consistently perform better than those with just one or two — each additional photo gives someone more reason to match with you.

<Card title="How to choose great profile photos" icon="camera" href="/profile-photos">
  - **Show Your Face:** Skip the sunglasses, hats, and masks.
  - **Go Solo:** Avoid group shots. Stand out on your own.
  - **Mix It Up:** Include both close-ups and full-body shots.
  - **Keep It Natural:** Ditch the bathroom mirror selfies. Natural lighting wins.
  - **Dress Your Best:** Wear clothes that fit well and show your style.
  - **Look Approachable:** Drop the tough look. Smile and look warm.
  - **Upload 5 to 8 Photos:** Give people a real sense of who you are.
</Card>
