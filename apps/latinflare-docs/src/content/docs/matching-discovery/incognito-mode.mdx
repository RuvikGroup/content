Incognito mode lets you use LatinFlare without showing as active or online to other users. It's useful when you want to browse privately.

## What incognito mode does

When incognito mode is on:

- You **won't appear as online** or active to other users
- Your **read receipts are hidden** — people won't know when you've read their messages
- You can still browse profiles, match, and message as normal

## How to enable incognito mode

Tap the **incognito toggle** on your home screen to switch it on or off.

![Screenshot 20260424 130914 LatinFlare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_130914_LatinFlare.jpg)

When active, the icon will change to indicate you're browsing privately.

## Incognito mode is a Premium feature

Incognito mode is available to Premium and VIP subscribers. If you're not subscribed, tapping the toggle will show you the subscription options.
