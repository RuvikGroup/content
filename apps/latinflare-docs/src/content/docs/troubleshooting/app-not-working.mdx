If LatinFlare isn't behaving as expected, these steps fix the vast majority of issues.

## Try these first

**1. Force close and reopen the app**

Close the app completely (don't just go to the home screen) and reopen it. This clears any temporary state that might be causing a problem.

**2. Check your internet connection**

Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to see if the issue is connection-related. If other apps also have trouble loading, the issue is likely your network.

**3. Restart your phone**

A full device restart solves a surprising number of app issues, especially after updates.

## Update the app

Running an outdated version can cause unexpected behaviour. Check the App Store or Google Play for any available updates and install them.

## Clear the app cache (Android)

<Steps>

1. **Open device Settings**

   Go to **Settings → Apps → LatinFlare**.

2. **Clear cache**

   Tap **Storage → Clear Cache**. This removes temporary files without deleting your account or data.

</Steps>

## Reinstall the app

If nothing else has worked, deleting and reinstalling the app gives you a clean install. Your account and matches are stored on our servers so nothing will be lost — just sign back in after reinstalling.

<Aside type="caution">
On iOS, make sure you remember your login method (Apple, Google, Facebook, or email) before uninstalling, so you can sign back in.
</Aside>

## Common specific issues

**Matches or messages not loading**

- Pull down to refresh on the matches or messages screen
- Check your internet connection
- Force close and reopen the app

**Photos not uploading**

- Make sure the app has permission to access your photo library (device Settings → LatinFlare → Photos)
- Check your connection — photo uploads require a stable internet connection
- Try a smaller file or a different photo

**Location not updating**

- Make sure location permission is enabled (device Settings → LatinFlare → Location)
- Try toggling your device's location/GPS off and back on

## Still having issues?

Contact our support team via the [contact form](https://latinflareapp.com/contact) with a description of the problem and your device model. Screenshots are always helpful.
