LatinFlare uses your location to show you people nearby. You can let the app use your device's GPS, or set your location manually to discover people in a different city or country.

## Using GPS (automatic)

When you first open the app, you'll be asked for location permission. Allowing this lets the app automatically keep your location up to date.

If you previously declined and want to enable it:

<Steps>

1. **Open your device settings**

   Go to your phone's **Settings** app.

2. **Find LatinFlare in your app list**

   Navigate to **Apps** (Android) or scroll to LatinFlare (iOS).

3. **Enable location access**

   Set location permission to **While using the app** or **Always**.

</Steps>

## Setting your location manually

You can set your location to anywhere in the world — useful if you're travelling, planning a trip, or want to connect with people in a specific city.

<Steps>

1. **Open location settings**

   Tap the location icon on the Explore page.\

       ![Arrow Image (3)](/images/arrow-image-\(3\).jpg)

2. **Search or pin your location**

   Use the search bar to find a city or address, or pan the map and tap to drop a pin on your desired location.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 122750 LatinFlare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_122750_LatinFlare.jpg)

3. **Confirm your location**

   Tap confirm to update your location. You'll now see people near your chosen location.

</Steps>

## Setting your search distance

You can adjust how far away you want to search from your location using the distance slider in your preferences. The default is 20 km.

<Aside type="tip">
If you're in a smaller city or rural area, try increasing your search distance to see more profiles.
</Aside>
