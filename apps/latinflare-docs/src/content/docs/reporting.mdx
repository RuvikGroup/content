If you encounter behaviour or content that violates our community guidelines, you can report it directly in the app. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team and we take appropriate action when a violation is confirmed.

## What you can report

- **Profiles** — inappropriate photos, fake or AI-generated pictures, impersonation, nudity, hate speech, or spam
- **Messages** — harassment, inappropriate content, nudity, hate speech, or spam

## How to report a profile

<Steps>

1. **Open the user's profile**

   Tap on the user's profile card to view their full profile.

2. **Open the actions menu**

   Tap the menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 133017 Asia Flare 1](/images/Screenshot_20260424_133017_LatinFlare-1.jpg)

3. **Tap Report User**

   Select **Report User** to open the report sheet.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 133430 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_133430_LatinFlare.jpg)

4. **Select a reason**

   Choose the reason that best describes the issue:

       | Reason | Description |
       | --- | --- |
       | No person | The photo does not contain a real person |
       | Nudity | The photo contains explicit content |
       | I find it offensive | The content is offensive or inappropriate |
       | Fake/AI Picture | The photo appears to be fake or AI-generated |
       | Racist | The content contains racist material |
       | Famous person / Celebrity | The profile is impersonating a public figure |
       | Spam | The profile is spam or a scam |
       | Other | Describe the issue in your own words |

</Steps>

## How to report a message

<Steps>

1. **Open the conversation**

   Navigate to your messages and open the chat containing the message you want to report.

2. **Long-press the message**

   Press and hold the message to reveal message options.

       ![Screenshot 20260424 133545 Asia Flare](/images/Screenshot_20260424_133545_LatinFlare.jpg)

3. **Tap Report Message**

   Select **Report Message** to open the report sheet.

4. **Select a reason and submit**

   Choose from: **Spam**, **Inappropriate**, **Harassment**, **Nudity**, or **Hate Speech** — then confirm your report.

</Steps>

## What happens after you report

- Our moderation team reviews the report
- If a rule violation is confirmed, we will ask the user to remove the content, or lock or suspend their account
- You will not be notified of the outcome to protect user privacy
- Reporting a user does not automatically block them — you can block them separately if needed

<Aside type="tip">
You can both report **and** block a user at the same time to immediately stop seeing their content while our team reviews your report.
</Aside>
